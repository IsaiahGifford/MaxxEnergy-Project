Contact form can be found at the bottom of the About Us page.

What should I do if I encounter issues with the website?

If you experience any issues, please reach out through the contact form on the Contact page.

Data Resources

What resources are available on the Data page?

The Data page has important resources like reports, analytics, and data about our energy production and use.

Who has access to modify data on the Data page?

Only admin users have the permissions to add or change data on the Data page.

How often is the data on the Data page updated?

The data is updated regularly, typically on a monthly basis, to ensure accuracy and relevance.

User Profile Management

How can I access my user profile?

To access your user profile, log in to the website and click on the profile icon located in the upper right corner.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email.

Can I change my email address associated with my profile?

Yes, you can update your email address in your user profile settings. Make sure to verify the new email address for security.
